ETL for NHL

Back in the summer of 2024, the redacted NHL Hockey Club launched a "Summer Analytics Challenge" inviting submissions to compete for a part-time paid gig with exposure to Hockey Operations and Analytics. While the request was more solicitous towards data science projects, I decided to throw my hat in the ring (rink?) regardless, and send off a proposal on the creation of an Enterprise Data Warehouse. Fast forward 18 months and, while I still technically haven't received a rejection, I figured it's probably safe to assume I'm not a winner -- so I decided to post it here.
