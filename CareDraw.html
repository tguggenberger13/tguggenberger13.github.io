CareDraw

The below was a submission for a grad school Health Informatics term project that came with the directive to “build something for health IT.”
I imagined a platform connecting patients and providers with independent (1099) phlebotomists for on-demand, in-home lab draws — a mix of logistics, care delivery, and a bit of startup daydreaming. Think DoorDash for blood, or Uber (D)x.
